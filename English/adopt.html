ABOUT CANARIONEJOS:

NON-PROFIT ANIMAL RESCUE, DEDICATED TO THE RESCUE OF ABANDONED RABBITS AND SMALL MAMMALS IN THE CANARY ISLANDS, SPECIFICALLY IN TENERIFE AND GRAN CANARIA

About

This Association was founded on December 23, 2016, thanks to a group of people concerned about the situation of animals on our island: abuse not pursued or condemned by the law. The purpose is to help these little forgotten ones, rabbits, and any small rodent in trouble. We operate based on foster homes where the doors are opened to them, and the dignity of these little 'broken toys' is restored. 90% of our income relies on private donations and markets; without them, we couldn't continue our mission. We are located in Gran Canaria, Canary Islands, Spain.
