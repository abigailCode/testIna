Common misconceptions/myths
Rabbits are rodents

They are actually lagomorphs

Rabbits are nocturnal

They are crepuscular, meaning they are most active at down and dusk

Rabbits are low maintenance

They are a lot of work and need no less care and attention than a dog or a cat

Cages? No, thanks

Rabbits need space to be able to run around, exercise and explore. Being enclosed in a cage prevent them from expressing natural behaviors and can cause health issues and stress
